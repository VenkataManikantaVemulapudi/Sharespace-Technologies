About Sharespace

Who We Are

Sharespace Technologies Pvt Ltd (Formerly Known as NCS Technologies) is an integrated service management and support company delivering comprehensive IT solutions across industries.

We specialize in Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC), Comprehensive AMC (CAMC), Facility Management Services (FMS), IT rentals, and turnkey deployments for CCTV, servers, storage, and enterprise networking.

  • Established: 2025
  • Customers served: 20+ and growing
  • Core services: IT Support, Servers & Storage, Networking, AV, CCTV, End-user Devices

Our certified engineers ensure uptime, security, and scalability, partnering with leading OEMs to deliver quality infrastructure and support.

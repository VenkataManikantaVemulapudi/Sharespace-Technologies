About Us

Who We Are

Sharespace Technologies Pvt Ltd (formerly NCS Technologies), established in 2019, is a reputed systems integrator and end‑to‑end IT solutions provider across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. We operate through multiple branches with a team of 1000+ qualified professionals.

We believe in transforming infrastructure, logistics, and security operations with robust, scalable, and secure IT. With a deep understanding of industry needs, we act as a trusted partner delivering efficient, modern solutions.

As a leading hybrid IT firm, we provide reliable services and genuine OEM-backed solutions—translating technology into real business outcomes.

Our teams bring deep expertise across hardware, networking, security, and cloud—aligning technology roadmaps with business goals so customers see measurable value from every project.

From assessment and design to deployment and managed services, we follow proven processes and quality checks that reduce risk, speed up delivery, and ensure consistent outcomes.

We partner closely with OEMs and distributors to maintain genuine inventory and reliable support SLAs, helping customers scale confidently and plan for future growth.

A 24×7 service desk and clear SLAs keep operations running smoothly, with proactive monitoring to resolve issues before they impact users.

Security and compliance are built into every engagement—covering hardening, patching, backup, and audit-ready documentation.

We enable teams with knowledge transfer, simple runbooks, and hands-on training so solutions remain sustainable long after go‑live.

Our Policy

Customer Satisfaction

Our top priority is customer satisfaction, and we strive to exceed our customers' expectations by providing quick and efficient service. We believe in providing timely updates and clear communication throughout the repair process to ensure our customers are fully informed and satisfied with the service they receive.

Quality Service

We are committed to providing quality service to our customers, and we ensure that all of our technicians are skilled and knowledgeable in the latest technologies and repair techniques. We use only genuine parts and software to ensure that our repairs are of the highest quality.

Timely Service

We understand that time is critical, and we aim to minimize the downtime our customers experience due to computer-related issues. We offer same-day service for most repairs and strive to complete our services as quickly and efficiently as possible.

Affordable Pricing

We believe that everyone deserves access to reliable and affordable computer services, and we strive to provide cost-effective solutions to our customers' computer-related issues. We offer competitive pricing and transparent cost estimates to ensure that our customers receive the best value for their money.

Data Privacy

We understand the importance of data privacy and are committed to protecting our customers' sensitive information. We ensure that all customer data is kept confidential and secure, and we use industry-standard security protocols to protect against data breaches.
